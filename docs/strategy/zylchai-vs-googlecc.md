# Comparison: Zylch AI vs. Google CC (2026)

## Executive Summary
While both tools act as "AI Concierges," **Zylch AI** focuses on managing business communications across multiple channels, whereas **Google CC** focuses on personal productivity and daily planning within Workspace.

---

## 1. Core Mission
*   **Zylch AI:** Acts as a **Business Communication Assistant**. Its primary goal is to resolve "communication chaos" by synthesizing meetings, emails, and phone calls into actionable intelligence for professionals.
*   **Google CC (Labs):** Acts as a **Personal Productivity Agent**. Its goal is to replace your "morning scroll" with a curated, personalized briefing that prepares you for the specific demands of your day.

## 2. Key Features & Capabilities
### Zylch AI
*   **Omnichannel Management:** Tracks business phone calls, team sharing, and contact intelligence alongside standard calendars.
*   **Live Business Demos:** Specifically designed for professional workflows like product team standups and client onboarding check-ins.
*   **Privacy-First:** Explicitly states that data is not shared for marketing purposes.

### Google CC
*   **"Your Day Ahead" Briefing:** Delivers a structured summary of your schedule, key tasks, and upcoming bills/deliveries to your Gmail inbox every morning.
*   **Drafting & Scheduling:** Can automatically prepare email drafts and attach relevant Google Calendar links for better coordination.
*   **Conversational Teaching:** Users can "teach" CC by replying to its briefings or emailing `[username]+cc@gmail.com` to add to-dos or correct information.

## 3. Ecosystem Integration
*   **Zylch AI:** A standalone platform designed to centralize various communication data points for business users.
*   **Google CC:** Deeply embedded in **Google Workspace**. It requires "Smart Settings" to be enabled and relies on native access to Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Drive.

5. Usage Scenarios
Use Zylch AI if: You are a project manager or sales lead who needs to sync cross-team communications and track client call intelligence across various platforms.
Use Google CC if: You have a busy personal schedule and want a simple morning email that tells you exactly when your meetings are, when your bills are due, and what you need to do next.
