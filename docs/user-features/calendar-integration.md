# Calendar Integration

**Schedule with context - Zylch knows who and why.**

---

## What It Does

Zylch integrates with Google Calendar to help you schedule meetings, add video links, and never lose context about why you're meeting.

---

## How to Use It

### Create Meetings

Just say what you need:

- "Schedule a call with Marco for Tuesday at 10"
- "Set up a meeting with Sofia next week"
- "Book 30 minutes with the team tomorrow"

Zylch will:
1. Check your availability
2. Create the event
3. Add attendees
4. Include context from your relationship

### Add Video Links

For remote meetings:

- "Add a Meet link to my meeting with Marco"
- "Schedule a video call with Sofia"

Zylch automatically generates Google Meet links.

### Find Meetings

- "What meetings do I have today?"
- "When am I meeting Marco next?"
- "Show me this week's schedule"

---

## Creating Events from Emails

When someone emails you suggesting a meeting:

**You:** "Create an invite for all participants at the proposed time with Meet link"

**Zylch will:**
1. Find the date/time in the email
2. Add all people from the email thread
3. Generate a Meet link
4. Create the calendar invite

---

## Meeting Context

Unlike a regular calendar, Zylch adds intelligence:

**Standard calendar:** "Meeting with Marco Ferrari - 10:00"

**With Zylch:**
```
Meeting with Marco Ferrari - 10:00
- Sales Director @ TechCorp
- Discussing: Q4 Proposal (email thread active)
- Last meeting: 2 weeks ago
- He prefers: Short meetings, direct communication
```

---

## Examples

### Quick Schedule
**You:** "Call with Marco Thursday 3pm"
**Zylch:** Creates "Call with Marco Ferrari" for Thursday 15:00

### From Email Context
**You:** "Set up the meeting Sofia proposed"
**Zylch:** Finds Sofia's email, extracts proposed time, creates event with all participants

### With Video Link
**You:** "Video meeting with the Milano team tomorrow 10am"
**Zylch:** Creates event, adds team members, generates Meet link

---

## Tips

- **Be natural** - "Coffee with Marco Friday" works
- **Zylch knows your contacts** - Just use names
- **Specify duration** - "30 minute call" or defaults to 1 hour
- **Add context** - "Meeting about the contract" saves what it's about
