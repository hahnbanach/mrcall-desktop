# Email Management

**Draft and find emails fast - in your own style.**

---

## What It Does

Zylch helps you write better emails faster, find any conversation instantly, and never lose track of important messages.

---

## How to Use It

### Draft Emails

Just tell Zylch what you want to say:

- "Write to Marco thanking him for the meeting"
- "Send a follow-up to Sofia about the project"
- "Remind Elena I'm waiting for the contract"

Zylch will:
1. Draft the email in your style
2. Show you the draft for review
3. Save it to Gmail drafts (when you approve)

### Find Emails

Search naturally:

- "Find emails about the Q4 budget"
- "What did Marco send last week?"
- "Show me unread from important contacts"

### Reply to Specific Emails

Reference emails by what you remember:

- "Reply to Marco's email about pricing"
- "For email #5, write a polite decline"
- "Answer Sofia's question about the deadline"

---

## Email Workflow

1. **You ask:** "Write a reminder to Marco about the proposal"
2. **Zylch drafts:** Shows you the email
3. **You review:** Make changes or approve
4. **Save draft:** "Save it" - saved to Gmail
5. **Send:** "Send it" - sends the email

### Editing Drafts

After Zylch creates a draft:
- Say "change the subject to..." for AI edits
- Say "edit" to open and edit manually
- Say "make it shorter" or "more formal" for tone changes

---

## Examples

### Quick Follow-up
**You:** "Follow up with Marco on the meeting"
**Zylch:** Drafts an email referencing your recent meeting, asking about next steps

### Finding Information
**You:** "What did I promise to send Elena?"
**Zylch:** Searches your emails and shows commitments you made

### Replying with Context
**You:** "Reply to Sofia saying yes to Thursday"
**Zylch:** Finds Sofia's email, drafts a reply confirming Thursday

---

## Your Email Style

Zylch learns how you write:
- **Length** - Short and direct, or detailed
- **Greetings** - "Hi Marco" vs "Dear Mr. Ferrari"
- **Sign-off** - "Best" vs "Cordiali saluti"
- **Language** - Matches the language you use

The more you use it, the more it sounds like you.

---

## Tips

- **Be specific** - "Remind about the contract" works better than "send email"
- **Reference context** - "About yesterday's meeting" helps Zylch understand
- **Review before sending** - Always check the draft
- **Teach Zylch** - Correct drafts and Zylch remembers for next time
