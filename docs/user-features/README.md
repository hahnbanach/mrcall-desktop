# What Zylch AI Can Do For You

Zylch AI is your personal intelligence assistant that helps you stay on top of all your communications and relationships.

---

## Your Capabilities at a Glance

### Know Everyone You Know
Ask Zylch about anyone in your network. Get their complete history across email, calendar, and phone - all in one place.

**Just ask:**
- "What's happening with Marco?"
- "Tell me about Sofia Bianchi"
- "When did I last talk to the team at TechCorp?"

[Learn more about Contact Intelligence →](./contact-intelligence.md)

---

### Email Made Easy
Draft emails in your style, find any conversation instantly, and never miss an important message.

**Just ask:**
- "Write a follow-up to Marco about the proposal"
- "Find emails about the Q4 budget"
- "What did I promise to send Elena?"

[Learn more about Email Management →](./email-management.md)

---

### Calendar with Context
Schedule meetings with full context. Zylch knows who you're meeting and what you've discussed.

**Just ask:**
- "Schedule a call with Marco for next Tuesday"
- "Add a Meet link to tomorrow's meeting"
- "What meetings do I have this week?"

[Learn more about Calendar Integration →](./calendar-integration.md)

---

### Your Daily Briefing
Start each day knowing exactly what needs your attention. Who to follow up with, what's overdue, and who you haven't talked to in a while.

**Commands:**
- `/sync` - Get your morning briefing
- `/gaps` - See relationship gaps that need attention

[Learn more about Daily Briefing →](./daily-briefing.md)

### Zylch Learns Your Style
The more you use Zylch, the better it gets. Corrections become automatic preferences.

**Examples:**
- Prefer short emails? Zylch remembers.
- Always CC your assistant? Zylch suggests it.
- Specific greeting for clients? Zylch uses it.

[Learn more about Memory & Learning →](./memory-learning.md)

## Quick Start

1. **Start Zylch**: Run the app
2. **Sync your data**: Type `/sync`
3. **Ask anything**: Just talk naturally!

**Pro tip:** You don't need special commands. Just ask in plain language - Zylch understands you.

## Getting Help

- Type `/help` to see all commands
- Type `/tutorial` for an interactive tour
- Just ask: "How do I...?" and Zylch will guide you
