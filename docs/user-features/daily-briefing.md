# Daily Briefing

**Know what needs your attention - every morning.**

---

## What It Does

Start each day with a clear picture of what matters. Zylch analyzes your emails, calendar, and relationships to show you exactly what needs attention.

---

## How to Use It

### Morning Sync

Type `/sync` to get your daily briefing:

```
/sync
```

You'll see:
- **Today's meetings** - What's on your calendar
- **Priority emails** - Important messages needing response
- **Follow-ups needed** - People waiting to hear from you
- **Relationship gaps** - Contacts going silent

### Relationship Gaps

Type `/gaps` to see who needs attention:

```
/gaps
```

Zylch identifies:
- **Meetings without follow-up** - You met but didn't follow up
- **Important emails without reply** - Urgent messages you haven't answered
- **Silent contacts** - People you usually talk to but haven't recently

---

## What Zylch Tracks

### Priority Emails
Emails that need your attention, ranked by:
- Sender importance (executives, key clients)
- Content urgency (deadlines, requests)
- Time waiting (how long without reply)

### Follow-up Needs
- Commitments you made ("I'll send you...")
- Questions asked to you
- Promises waiting on others

### Relationship Gaps
- Key contacts you haven't engaged with
- Meetings that happened without follow-up
- Changes in communication patterns

## Example Briefing

```
Good morning! Here's your briefing for Monday, November 25:

TODAY'S CALENDAR (3 meetings):
- 10:00 - Call with Marco Ferrari (Q4 Review)
- 14:00 - Team sync
- 16:30 - Sofia Bianchi (Project kickoff)

NEEDS YOUR ATTENTION:
1. Elena Verdi - waiting for contract (3 days)
2. TechCorp team - proposal feedback requested
3. Giovanni - asked about pricing Thursday

RELATIONSHIP GAPS:
- Marco Bianchi: Last contact 3 weeks ago (usually weekly)
- Design team: Meeting last Tuesday, no follow-up sent

QUICK WINS:
- Reply to Elena (she just needs a yes/no)
- Send Marco the doc you promised
```

---

## Customizing Your Briefing

Zylch learns what matters to you:

- **Tell Zylch** - "Giovanni is low priority" or "Always include Elena"
- **Correct it** - "That email isn't important" teaches Zylch
- **Set preferences** - "Don't show newsletter senders in gaps"

Over time, your briefing gets more relevant.

---

## Tips

- **Run `/sync` every morning** - Make it a habit
- **Check `/gaps` weekly** - Catch relationship drift early
- **Act on one thing** - Pick the most important and do it
- **Teach Zylch** - Correct what's not useful

| Command | What It Does |
|---------|--------------|

| `/sync 7` | Briefing based on last 7 days |
| `/gaps` | Relationship gaps only |
