# Contact Intelligence

**Know everyone you know - across all channels.**

---

## What It Does

Zylch gives you a complete picture of any person in your network. Instead of searching through emails, calendar, and phone separately, just ask - and get everything in one place.

---

## How to Use It

### Ask About Anyone

Simply ask Zylch about a person:

- "What's happening with Marco Ferrari?"
- "Tell me about Sofia"
- "Who is Giovanni Rossi?"

Zylch will show you:
- Contact details (email, phone, company)
- Recent conversations (emails, meetings)
- Relationship context (client, partner, colleague)
- What's pending (emails to reply, upcoming meetings)

### Find People by Context

You can also search by relationship or company:

- "Who do I know at TechCorp?"
- "Show me my partners"
- "Who haven't I talked to in a month?"

### See the Full Picture

For each contact, Zylch combines:
- **Email history** - What you've discussed
- **Calendar** - Past and upcoming meetings
- **Phone** - Call history (with MrCall)
- **Your notes** - What you've told Zylch about them

---

## Example

**You ask:** "What's happening with Marco?"

**Zylch responds:**
```
Marco Ferrari
Sales Director @ TechCorp Italia
marco.ferrari@techcorp.it | +39 333 123 4567

Recent Activity:
- Email 2 days ago: "Re: Q4 Proposal" (needs reply)
- Meeting tomorrow: Follow-up call (10:00 with Meet link)
- Last phone call: 1 week ago (12 minutes)

Context:
- Active deal in progress
- You committed to send updated pricing
- He prefers morning calls
```

---

## Tips

- **Be natural** - Just ask like you're asking a colleague
- **Use nicknames** - If you call them "Marco", Zylch learns that
- **Add context** - "Marco from the Milano deal" helps find the right person
- **Save contacts** - Say "save Marco to contacts" to keep enriched info
